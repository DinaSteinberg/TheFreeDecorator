Your Dream Kitchen
Is On Its Way!

The kitchen is more the family room than the living room. It's where all the fun happens
- the baking parties, family meal times... It's where the mom is perpetualy found, it's where
the most mess happens, and the most memories. You want your kitchen to be a comfortable and
easily navigatable. And while in other rooms organization is only important, organization in
your kitchen is key! If you don't have a set place for certain pots, certain silverware, or your
mixer, no food will get made. There's also the added element that if you don't put away your food
carefully, the bugs will get to it. So here are some tips for how to organize your kitchen, what kind
of furniture you should use, and how to arrange it all.
