The Free Decorator

Designing a house can be overwhelming. Whether you're building from scratch, renovating,
or just ready for a face lift, this website will provide you with some great resources
to find good, reliable information and to get some ideas of how to decorate. Decorating
can get overwhelming, but you have to focus on the good and fun parts.

This website will take you through all the essentials. The first folder, The Decorating
process, deals with basics like color schemes, budgets and optimizing your space. This
is especially helpful for small houses or studio apartments.

The second folder goes through all the essential of the living room and dining room. This
is incredibly helpful as we included tips such as the best wood for your dining room table,
choosing couches and chairs, and accesories.

The third folder deals with kitchen essentials, such as cabinets, tables, and organization.
When you're done with that page, you kitchen will look amazing and organized!

The last folder deals with all things bedroom related, such as picking the right linen, organizing
your room, and choosing great furniture.

Good Luck on your design journey!
