Accesorising your Living Room

Ok so you have your big pieces of furniture. Now here comes the fun! Putting together
accesories can completley change a room. A room that started off looking stiff and untouchable
can become warm and inviting with some well-placed accessories.Accesorising can
tedious and frustrating but with the tips below, the process will be much smoother.
Don't let yourself get bogged down by the details. Get excited, because this is the fun
part!

DO NOT accessorize a room with too many little things.
A few or even several are okay, especially if they are grouped together.
They can add detail to a vignette or a stack of books or a basket, but
too many little decorations scattered about can end up looking like clutter.
A trio of smaller items can look quite charming together on a little table
or a stool or tucked into a corner. Just don’t over do the amount of small
grouping of small things in one room!

The bigger the piece of furniture the bigger the accessories should be!
Think BIG when accesorising! Go for the big houseplant, the statement chandelier,
or the large lamp. Sometimes one large item on the side can look nicer than
a lot of small pieces.

Make a statement! Accessorizing your home is all about letting YOUR personality
shine through in your decor! Use what you love. I think people like embellishing and
accessorizing so much because they can build a layer of decor that is personal and
creatively theirs!

Layer, layer, layer! Layering decor is another way to beautifully accessorize
your home! It can be a bit of an art and most of us need to spend a little time
working on it to achieve a pleasing-to-the-eye look. Layering accessories must
LOOK effortless, but it’s not at all!

Accessories should be all about bringing interest, beauty and even function
into a room. Think about textures, heights, materials, balance, and size when
layering and embellishing. Use something organic too! All the decor layers
you add to your home should work together!

Think of layering as “playing with what you have”. Play with decor in your home
that you love and think might work well together. Accessorize an area, stand
back and look at it. Edit and add and change things up. I often have to walk
away from something I’m working on and come back to it later with a “fresh eye”.
Layering is so worth the effort and time it takes to get it right!

Another one of my favorite ways to accessorize is to create a fabulous vignette!
I love to group things together (big and small and in between) in a basket, tray
or crate. The synergy of a vignette like this makes one big decor statement.
The same elements and rules used in layering also apply to a vignette. A vignette
is a grouping of items corralled together to make one big eye-pleasing
(said that before too) decorative “story”. Even a kitchen table can look
beautiful with a vignette made from its wares!
I may have left the best for last. Pillows are like jewelry to a room!
Think of them as little gems that add amazing beauty of a room! A room
just does not seem complete without some well chosen pillows!
I love that I can change up pillows so easily! They can totally change
the look of the entire room! Same sofa, same mirror, same lamp, same anchor
pillows and rug as above, but very different look! When you
accessorize your next room, start with the pillows!
