Color Schemes

How you feel when you walk into your house depends on the colors around you. A homey interior
will make your shoulders relax and will instantly calm you down. A bright colorful home will help
your perk up as soon as you enter your home. A color scheme is a very important first step when planning your home.

Not everyone starts knowing which colors they want. But that's ok! You can gather clippings and pictures with
colors that pull you, whether it's a room you see in a friend's insta post, or it's a forest
panorama with colors that blend in a way that pulls you. Put these pictures and clippings together
in a portfolio and look for patterns. You should notice some colors that keep on popping up
in the pictures. You can look at the chart below to see which other colors can go well with
"your" colors.
